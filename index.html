Meet Australia’s most powerful Dual Cab Ute resetting the bar in its class to unrivalled territory. Since 1955, Harrop has been a leader in engineering performance, a specialist in supercharger development and legendary with high performance Holden and GM V8 platforms. Superado features an iconic LSA engine with Harrop TVS2650 Supercharger System mated to a 6L80 6 speed Automatic transmission. Superado is available in two dynamic packages, designed to dominate the streets or the beaten track for adventure.
